What Is Budin?

Another popular dish not only in El Salvador, but in many other latin countries, budin is essentially banana bread pudding.

Ingredients

  • Banana
  • Bolio
  • Milk
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. First mix all the ingredients
  2. Pour batter onto a pan to bake in
  3. Bake
  4. Remove form oven and allow it to cool down
  5. Enjoy
